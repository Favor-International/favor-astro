# Homepage Bands

Modeled on Destiny Rescue. Full-width visual bands, stacked top to
bottom, each with a single purpose and one CTA.

Order is sticky. Do not rearrange without a logged decision.

## Band 1: Hero

- **Purpose:** Grab attention. State the promise. Set the stakes.
- **Content:**
  - H1: the partner-as-hero hook (see Open Question Q1 for the
    specific phrasing; example draft: *"You can send a pastor where
    others will not go."*)
  - Subhead: one sentence, the cost or specific outcome.
  - Primary CTA: **Become a Favor Partner**
  - Secondary link: **See the field**
- **Visual:** Full-bleed field photo or a 10-15 second silent loop
  (autoplay only if no audio, with `prefers-reduced-motion` fallback
  to still). Left-side scrim for legibility.
- **Trust strip:** Below the hero copy, a thin row of trust marks:
  ECFA logo, 501(c)(3) badge, years operating, nations served.

## Band 2: The signature phrase

- **Purpose:** Brand anchor. Sets the emotional baseline.
- **Content:**
  - Display type: **"Where others will not go."**
  - One paragraph (max 60 words): what this means in practice.
- **Visual:** Sparse. White background. Dark green type. No photo.

## Band 3: The four ministry pillars

- **Purpose:** Show the work at a glance. Route users to IMPACT.
- **Content:** Four cards, each with icon, name of pillar, one-line
  description, and a "Learn more" link to the pillar page.
- **Visual:** Light sage background (#c5ccc2 at 20% mix with white).
- **Blocked by:** Open Question Q4 (define the four pillars).

## Band 4: Real transformation stories

- **Purpose:** Proof. Story-forward.
- **Content:** Three featured stories pulled from the Stories CMS.
  Each card: photo, name and country, one-sentence outcome, "Read the
  story" link.
- **Visual:** White background. Cards have soft shadow and 8px radius.
- **Manual override:** A featured story can be pinned in the CMS to
  keep it in slot 1.

## Band 5: Impact numbers

- **Purpose:** Quantify the work.
- **Content:** 3 or 4 large stats in a row.
  - Example slots: pastors trained, nations served, churches planted,
    Bibles distributed.
- **Visual:** Dark green background (#2b4d24), light gold accent on
  numbers, white labels.
- **Source line:** "FY2025 annual report" or equivalent at the bottom
  of the band in 12px sage.

## Band 6: Become a Favor Partner (dedicated band)

- **Purpose:** Monthly giving primary conversion moment.
- **Content:**
  - H2: **"Become a Favor Partner."**
  - One paragraph: what monthly partnership unlocks for the field.
  - Tile selector with three suggested monthly amounts (e.g., $25,
    $50, $100) and a "Choose your own" option.
  - Primary CTA button: **Partner Monthly**
  - Trust line: "Your gift is tax-deductible. Favor International is
    ECFA-accredited."
- **Visual:** Gold band (#e1a730) with dark text. Photo of a field
  leader to the right or as a background with strong scrim.

## Band 7: Pray with us

- **Purpose:** Catch the prayer-first visitor. Lower-friction action.
- **Content:**
  - H2: **"You can pray where they cannot reach."**
  - Two columns: brief description + CTA to the 30-Day Prayer Guide
    PDF download.
- **Visual:** Light tan background. Field photo of a prayer gathering.

## Band 8: Go (vision trips)

- **Purpose:** Surface the Go path. Optional but recommended.
- **Content:**
  - H2: **"See it for yourself."**
  - Upcoming vision trips: 2-3 cards with destination, date window,
    and "Learn more".
- **Visual:** White background. Photo-forward cards.

## Band 9: Field updates / blog teaser

- **Purpose:** Show the site is alive. Surface fresh content.
- **Content:** Latest 3 field updates or stories from the CMS.

## Band 10: Newsletter signup

- **Purpose:** Capture email from non-givers.
- **Content:**
  - H2: **"Get the field report."**
  - Single-field email input + submit.
  - One-line privacy note.
- **Visual:** Light sage background. Minimal.

## Band 11: Footer

- **Purpose:** Trust, navigation, legal.
- **Content:**
  - Logo + tagline
  - Address (Valrico, FL)
  - ECFA badge
  - 501(c)(3) line
  - Sitemap by section
  - Social links
  - Copyright line
- **Visual:** Dark green background (#2b4d24), white text, gold accents.

## Conversion notes

- The **Become a Favor Partner** band is the highest-priority band on
  the page after the hero. It must be visible without scrolling past
  more than two full sections.
- ECFA trust marks must appear in the hero trust strip *and* in the
  footer.
- The "Pray" band is intentionally low-friction. No name capture.
  Just download.
